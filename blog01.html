Attitude of Gratitude.

No words can explain how grateful I am to be an intern in New York a second time. The energy of this city fuels my creativity--and in working at a creative agency, I hope to cycle that energy right back. Although there have been times where I've missed home (both Manila and San Francisco) I am exactly where I am supposed to be.
