Daniel's: No Sauce, No Problem

ACTIVATION

How do you promote an up-and-coming restaurant known for serving exculsively sauceless food?

This was a fun, icebreaker project created during my internship at Ogilvy NY (as well as a work in progress!)
The Ask was to shortly conceptualize a campaign on a personal trait from a fellow intern--in this case, my friend Daniel, who hates the concept of sauce in food. So I built a restaurant solely based off of this fun fact of his, and created this idea of an activation filled with free samples for potential customers to discover the sauceless sensation.

This project was fun in that I got to brainstorm both a brand and a campaign. It was also a good excercise in copywriting, which is a skill I am determined to exercise.
