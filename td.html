TD Bank Summer Reading Program

SOCIAL, PRINT, OOH

How do you grab the attention of parents to promote TD Bank's Summer Reading Program?

These social posts were created during my internship at TBWA\Chiat\Day NY, with Louis Prenaud (AD) and Aste Gutierrez (CD).
Responsibilities included selecting photos of children, designing the logo, mocking the books onto the photographs, and color correcting.

Goal: Promote TD Bank's summer program, which encourages children to read ten books in exchange for getting a bank account started with $10.

Idea: Use humor in showing kids trying to read books outside their age range.

Outcome: The posts were client approved, and are posted on the TD Bank Instagram account.
