Mission Permission: Teach Kids Personal Space

Product Design



Goal: Design a product or campaign against sexual harassment.

Strategy: Start them young--introduce the concept of consent to children in a fun, understandable medium. The result, the Mission Permission card game, introduces the concept of consent and physical boundaries to children before their mindset is influenced by portrayals of sexual violence in popular media.
The game puts a spin on a regular matching card game, and incorporates an astronaut theme with diverse and non-gender specific characters and colors.


This was created at the University of California in Davis, during Winter Quarter 2019.



Creative Direction: Emilia Tongson

Design, Strategy: Sierra Sparre, Alexis Cruz-Rosales

Account: Brantlee Griffith



Outcome: The card game was accepted into the Design Museum in Davis, California. The product is also still currently in testing mode.
